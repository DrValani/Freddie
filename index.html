Plot your trek

Paste the output of your pathfinding in the textarea below to see your robot's journey! The map may be rotated compared to what you expect. The expected format are separate x,y coordinate pairs per line wrapped in parentheses. These may be surrounded by other text. For example:
pos=(123.345, 7986.909) height=5094.645
or
3,820m (5252.494098, 1747.964328) step: 4.756804
Would be valid input formats.
